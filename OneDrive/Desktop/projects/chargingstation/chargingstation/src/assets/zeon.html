Why partner with us?

A typical fast charging stop for an electric vehicle would take between 20 to 60 minutes. This would be an ideal me for travellers to take a break for coffee, food and shopping. We are partnering with office campuses, restaurants, retail shopping centres, hotels, fuel stations, cinema halls, parking lot operators, and other stakeholders to make it easier for everyone to drive electric.

As our location partners your business premises will become the preferred choice for Electric car owners. We will install EV chargers in your customer car parks and all costs related to this will be on us. You may also choose to become our Franchise by investing in the charging infrastructure at your premises. All travellers wanting for a quick EV charge will become your customers inevitably.
